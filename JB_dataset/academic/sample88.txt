Dear Informatics UG1 and UG2 students,

Are you interested in studying abroad in your 3rd year?

We will have the following event, where you can meet the students who
did studying-abroad programmes last year. There will be short
presentations by the students, and a Q&A session. 

  Event name:   Informatics Go Abroad Meeting
  Date & Time:  Wednesday, 23rd October, 17:10-18:00
  Venue:        Appleton Tower - Lecture Theatre 4 (AT-LT4)

by the Edinburgh Global (aka International Office) on the same day at
12:30-16:30 in McEwan Hall, Bristo Square.

You can find information on studying abroad from the following links:
  http://web.inf.ed.ac.uk/infweb/student-services/ito/students/year2/studying-abroad
