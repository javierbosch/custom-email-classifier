Apply for the AWS Educate Student Ambassador Program	 
Hi Javier,

As part of your new AWS Educate membership, you're eligible to apply for the AWS Educate Student Ambassador Program, offering students recognition, professional development, and benefits for helping their peers learn about cloud computing.

As members of the program, AWS Educate Student Ambassadors will receive the support they need to help grow the AWS Educate community at their school and a host of benefits, including:


*	Incremental AWS Promotional Credit
*	Access to AWS Summits, conferences, and industry events (when applicable)
*	Early access, under NDA, to select AWS services
*	Limited edition AWS Educate Student Ambassador Program branded items
	
*	Quarterly AWS tech talks on in-demand AWS services such as AWS RoboMaker and AWS DeepLens
*	The opportunity to develop public speaking, technical writing, community leadership, and networking skills
*	Training and mentorship from Amazon employees

If selected, Student Ambassadors are expected to champion AWS and AWS Educate at their schools through presentations, social media, hackathons and other events.

The application period ends on April 2, 2020 at 11:59 PM PST. Use the link below to apply.
