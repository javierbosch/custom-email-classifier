Dear Javi,

The Careers in Engineering Fair is today! There will be 30 employers at the Sanderson Building keen to speak to you about placements, graduate roles and other work opportunities.

When: Today! Wednesday 30 October, 1.30pm – 4.30pm
Where: Ground Floor, Sanderson Building, King’s Buildings

You will have the opportunity to meet a mix of both well-known large global employers and smaller local organisations. There will be opportunities for students and graduates from all engineering disciplines as well as for students from other STEM backgrounds.

For the full list of exhibitors and the fair guide please check out the Careers in Engineering Fair website <https://www.ed.ac.uk/careers/about-us/what-we-do/events/our-flagship-events/careersinengineering-fair-2019> .

Please note that one third of exhibitors are new to the fair this year so even if you have attended in previous years we recommend coming along again.

No need to register - just come along and find out where your degree could take you!
