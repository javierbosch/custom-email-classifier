[Students] Part time work available
---------------
Oodls is looking for a part-time UX/UI designer, preferably somebody with experience developing front-end in React. For the application, make a proposal to describe how you would improve our website: https://oodls.io/ while thinking about user flow speed and usability. 

You can read the job description on https://jobs.smartrecruiters.com/oodls/743999692609417-freelance-ux-ui-designer?fbclid=IwAR1s3cRISye73ge2mODxZDvyD96Q8-YazGrW6_W8dhDBM4q9K0N1PedZSHQ and submit your proposals there after clicking “I’m interested”, filling out the form, and pasting your proposal under the “Message to Hiring Manager” section.

We are preferably looking for someone with industry experience but open to ALL levels of talent.
