Advancing your career - a session for mature students

Wednesday 26 February, 6-7pm, Online 

Did you start your undergraduate degree aged 21 or over? If so, this session will cover content and issues which are relevant for you as a mature student. We will cover: tips for advancing and/or changing career; how to write and talk about previous experience/life in applications; and how to effectively use online platforms such as LinkedIn. The session will end with a Q&A.

This is an online session via the Collaborate platform - access the link on booking.
