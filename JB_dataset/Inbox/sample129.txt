[Students] Mini ILCR event

The International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR) is one of the most prestigious in machine learning. EdIntelligence will bring to you some of the UoE research accepted to the conference.


When: 24th of March <x-apple-data-detectors://1> 
Time: 18:00 - 19:30 <x-apple-data-detectors://2> 
Where: Appleton Tower Lecture Theatre 5


The presented papers are:

Physics-as-Inverse-Graphics: Unsupervised Physical Parameter Estimation from Video (Miguel Jaques, Michael Burke, Timothy Hospedales)

Presented by Miguel Jaques

Generative Ratio Matching Networks (Akash Srivastava, Kai Xu, Michael U. Gutmann, Charles Sutton)

Presented by Kai Xu

BlockSwap: Fisher-guided Block Substitution for Network Compression on a Budget (Jack Turner, Elliot J. Crowley, Michael O'Boyle, Amos Storkey, Gavin Gray)

Presented by Elliot Crowley



The event will conclude with drinks and nibbles. More information you can find on the Facebook event: https://www.facebook.com/events/543523593188769/.
