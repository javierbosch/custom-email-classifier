Kings Buildings Bus Stop changes

The bus stop at King's Buildings has moved to outside Murchison House. The KB Shuttle Bus and the 41 Service now both use this stop. The bus stop outside the Roger Land Building is no longer in use. Find out more about transport to and from our campuses by visiting the Transport & Parking webpages <https://mailings.ed.ac.uk/27JU-1J2DE-9FSYIA-ZUD98-1/c.aspx> .
