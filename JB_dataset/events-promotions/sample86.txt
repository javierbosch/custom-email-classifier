Student Experience Grants - project funding for students

Supercharge your ideas by applying for an alumni-funded project grant. Student Experience Grants are one-off contributions of up to £5,000 to support unique initiatives that will enhance students’ social, academic or cultural development. Applications open 17 October. Find out more <https://mailings.ed.ac.uk/27JU-1H8X2-9FSYIA-YBWRQ-1/c.aspx> .

New Online support ‘SilverCloud’ has arrived at the University 

SilverCloud is a free suite of interactive online 
cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT) programmes on anxiety, depression, stress and body image. It joins the other excellent mental health support such as ‘Big White Wall’ and the award winning ‘Feeling Good App’ on the Student Counselling homepage. <https://mailings.ed.ac.uk/27JU-1H8X2-9FSYIA-YBWJL-1/c.aspx>
