Let's Talk podcast

Let's Talk is a new podcast series bringing together staff and students from across the University to have thought-provoking discussions about mental health. Each episode focuses on a different mental health topic - usually something many of us will experience at some point but are often afraid to talk about. Listen now <https://mailings.ed.ac.uk/27JU-1I85F-9FSYIA-Z5Y4X-1/c.aspx> .
