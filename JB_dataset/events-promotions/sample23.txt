New Health & Wellbeing Centre: coming soon


Our fully accessible Centre based at 7 Bristo Square will open this March. It will house the Counselling and Disability Services, the University Health Centre and Pharmacy. 

The building will offer more consultation rooms and a wellbeing lounge for you to relax in.
Find out more <https://mailings.ed.ac.uk/27JU-1JV50-9FSYIA-10FLGD-1/c.aspx>

Gain academic credit for your summer experience

First or second-year undergraduate? SLICCs give you the opportunity to earn 20 credits for your involvement in co- or extra-curricular experiences during your summer vacation. Build your skills, develop your employability, have an amazing summer, and get credits for it! 

Register your interest <https://www.ed.ac.uk/employability/slicc/apply/register-your-interest-for-summer-sliccs>

Jazz Club Time Machine

The wait is over. Jazz Club Time Machine is here. Friday 27 March.
