The grant provides up to £500 of funding to student groups or societies hoping to develop and implement projects around climate change, circular economy and reuse, food and drink, and community. Apply by 4th December <https://mailings.ed.ac.uk/27JU-1HZOY-9FSYIA-YYH8E-1/c.aspx> .
