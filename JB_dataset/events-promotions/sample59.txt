One more week to apply for the Clinton Foundation’s CGI U programme

CGI U is a year-long student programme aimed at bringing together the next generation of entrepreneurs to come up with innovative solutions to some of the world’s most pressing challenges. The programme is open to all students. Find out more <https://mailings.ed.ac.uk/27JU-1HRHY-9FSYIA-YREDW-1/c.aspx> .

November is Disability History Month

Disability History Month is an opportunity to celebrate and commemorate the individuals and events which have shaped disabled communities, as well as our collective history. Throughout November, the Students’ Association is organising a number of events and activities you can get involved with. More information <https://mailings.ed.ac.uk/27JU-1HRHY-9FSYIA-YRE5L-1/c.aspx> .
