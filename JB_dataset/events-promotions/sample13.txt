Who will represent you?

Voting in #StudentElections2020 is now open! Candidates will be out campaigning so make sure to chat to them to find out why they are running. Have your say and decide who will represent you. You can vote through MyEd <http://www.myed.ed.ac.uk>  or on the Students’ Association’s website <https://www.eusa.ed.ac.uk/yourvoice/elections/submit/> 

 <https://www.eusa.ed.ac.uk/activities/global/globalfund/> 	

Apply now to the Global Community Fund

Created by Edinburgh Global and Students’ Association, the Fund provides grants up to £300, to help with the creation of inclusive events. The Global Community Fund aims to encourage conversations on global issues, promote global citizenship and celebrate Edinburgh’s diverse community. Apply now <https://www.eusa.ed.ac.uk/activities/global/globalfund/>
