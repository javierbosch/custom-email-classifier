This Saint Andrew's Day (Scotland's Patron Saint) <https://dmtrk.net/L8C-6L6PM-WO2Y99-3U21YZ-1/c.aspx>  we have a full night of Scottish cheer <https://dmtrk.net/L8C-6L6PM-WO2Y99-3U21YZ-1/c.aspx>  lined up for you including food, drink and dancing! Here's how it'll go....




Start the evening by trying Scotland’s most famous cuisine, haggis*, neeps and tatties! (haggis, turnips and potatoes) <https://dmtrk.net/L8C-6L6PM-WO2Y99-3U21YZ-1/c.aspx> , served in the New Amphion. We recommend you pair it with a 'dram' (glass) of Scotland's world famous whisky. 




Work it all off by getting involved in the ceilidh (traditional Scottish music and dancing) <https://dmtrk.net/L8C-6L6PM-WO2Y99-3U21YZ-1/c.aspx> , taking place in the Debating Hall. Trust us, you will love it!




*Vegetarian haggis will be available! <https://dmtrk.net/L8C-6L6PM-WO2Y99-3U21YZ-1/c.aspx>
